Methods & sources

The plates are exact integer matrices. The individual constructions are classical; the system here is the composition — an operator algebra over an algebraic-number seed registry, graded by one transferable invariant. No new mathematics is claimed.

how each number is computed
  • Characteristic polynomial & determinant — Faddeev–LeVerrier (integer coefficients for integer matrices, O(n⁴)). Householder, The Theory of Matrices in Numerical Analysis.
  • Eigenvalues — Durand–Kerner / Weierstrass simultaneous iteration on the characteristic polynomial, O(n²) per step.
  • Mahler measure — M(p) = |aₙ|·∏ max(1, |λᵢ|); the char-poly is monic so M = ∏ over eigenvalues outside the unit circle. Entropy of the associated ℤ-action (Lind–Schmidt–Ward, Invent. Math. 1990); Lehmer's problem remains open.
where the matrices come from
  • Companion of a monic integer minimal polynomial — eigenvalues are the Galois conjugates; non-monic ZFP values (4x²−3, 9x²−9x+1, …) are excluded because they are not algebraic integers.
  • Kronecker ⊗ / direct sum ⊕ / commutator [A,B] — products give pairwise eigenvalue products; commutators are trace-free by construction (a live check).
  • Cartan matrices Aₙ, Dₙ, E₈ — Bourbaki, Lie Groups and Lie Algebras. det Aₙ = n+1, det Dₙ = 4, det E₈ = 1 (E₈ is unimodular; Conway & Sloane, SPLAG).
  • Fibonacci / Lucas circulant — eigenvalues are the DFT of the first row (Davis, Circulant Matrices); the golden row connects to the bloom's quasiperiodic floor.
  • Frustrated ring Laplacian — signed ring (alternating ±1), D̄ − A → positive-semidefinite signed Laplacian (singular exactly when balanced); the matrix form of antiphase Kuramoto coupling (Harary balance; signed-network synchronization).
  • Random (seeded) — mulberry32 PRNG, reproducible from the seed; the control family against which the structured Mahler grading is read.
